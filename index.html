"Phono" n. Sound. Vibrations that travel through the air or another medium and can be heard when they reach a person's or animal's ear.
"Phonia" adj. denoting a type of plug, and the corresponding socket, used with audio and video equipment, in which one conductor is cylindrical and the other is a central prong that extends beyond it.
